About DOC.PROFILE / v2.1

Cédric Godin is a filmmaker, director and creative producer working across international broadcast production and original IP development.

His career began in live performance and touring productions before expanding into technical direction and audiovisual coordination for international broadcasters and media organisations including Sky News, ARTE, TV5, Bloomberg, NBC Universal, the EBU, Roland Garros and Eurovision.

Alongside broadcast work, he develops and directs original documentaries and fiction projects exploring personal narratives, cultural movements and psychological themes. His work includes X Art, Who Is Taki and the award-winning feature PTSD.

Today, his primary focus is the development of original intellectual properties for television, animation and film, building narrative worlds designed for international audiences and long-form storytelling.

Alongside broadcast work, he develops original film, television and animation projects focused on scalable narrative worlds and international audiences.
